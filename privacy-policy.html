Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

7 Journal ("we", "our", or "the app") is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your journal entries are private and belong to you.

Data Collection

What we collect:

  • Journal entries (stored locally on your device or in your iCloud account)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Anonymous usage analytics (crash reports, feature usage)

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your name or contact information
  • The content of your journal entries on our servers
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers

Data Storage

Your journal entries are stored:

  • Locally on your device, protected by your device passcode
  • In iCloud (if enabled) using Apple's encrypted CloudKit service

We do not have access to your journal content. Only you can read your entries.

AI Processing

When you use voice transcription or AI analysis features:

  • Audio is sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription
  • Text may be sent to OpenAI's GPT API for analysis
  • OpenAI's data retention policies apply (see openai.com/privacy)
  • We do not store your audio or transcripts on our servers

Optional Encryption

7 Journal offers end-to-end encryption for your entries. When enabled:

  • Entries are encrypted using AES-256 before storage
  • Encryption keys are stored in your device's Secure Enclave
  • Keys are not synced to iCloud

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud - For optional cloud sync
  • OpenAI - For voice transcription and AI analysis
  • Apple StoreKit - For subscription management

Children's Privacy

7 Journal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
